Order Invoices

This sections allows yout to customize the invoices which are automatically produced when a customer places an order. The invoice will not automatically be sent to the customer by default, but you can use the Order Notifications to set up automatic Order Notification Emails that have the invoice attached. Alternatively, you could create the invoice yourself via the individual order section and then manually email it to the customer.

This sections allows you to customize your invoices in a number of ways. You can select a layout, choose a color, upload your logo, translate the text, and more.
