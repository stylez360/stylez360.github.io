Categories

Categories are an important aspect of your webstore and help your customers navigate around your store. You can edit your categories in numerous ways:

  • Double click a category to edit the category.
  • Click and hold a category to drag it around.
  • Right click a category to:
    • Edit,
    • Show/ Hide,
    • Or, delete.

Orphaned Categories Found

Orphaned categories are categories that do not have a parent category or they were set to delete but did not get deleted.

Before you can have full access to your categories you first need to sort these categories by clicking the "Sort" button below:
